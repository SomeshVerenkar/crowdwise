### Q: Can I visit Taj Mahal at night?
**A:** Yes, but only on full moon nights and 2 days before/after (5 nights per month). Limited to 400 visitors. Book 24 hours in advance. Magical experience but higher cost (‚Çπ750).

### Q: Is it worth visiting in monsoon season?
**A:** Absolutely! July-August sees 70% fewer crowds. Bring umbrella, wear waterproof shoes. The marble gets even more beautiful when wet. Gardens are lush green.

### Q: How long should I plan to spend at Taj Mahal?
**A:**

**A:** Food, tobacco, cameras with large lenses (DSLR allowed, professional equipment not), tripods, drones, books, chargers.

### Q: Can I bring kids?

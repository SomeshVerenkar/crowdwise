The Story

India sees over 2 billion domestic tourist visits per year, yet travellers have had no reliable way to know how crowded a destination will be before they arrive. The result: ruined weekends, missed photo opportunities, and avoidable stress at some of the world's most iconic sites.

CrowdWise India was built to solve this. Launched in early 2026, the platform covers 359 destinations across all 38 Indian states and Union Territories — from the Taj Mahal at Agra to lesser-known gems in the Northeast — providing crowd-level forecasts without requiring any registration, payment, or data from the user.

The platform runs a multi-signal prediction algorithm weighing time-of-day patterns, day of week, month-level seasonality, Indian public holidays, Wikipedia page-view trends, hotel demand signals, and live weather. It generates crowd levels updated in real time, entirely in the user's browser — no server round-trips, no loading spinners.

CrowdWise India is 100% free, no login required and deploys as a static site served globally via Cloudflare's CDN — meaning it loads fast from anywhere in India on any device or connection speed.

Full Press Description
CrowdWise India is an independent travel intelligence platform that solves one of domestic tourism's most persistent frustrations: arriving at a destination to find it overwhelmed with crowds. Covering 359 tourist destinations across all 38 Indian states and Union Territories — from the Taj Mahal in Uttar Pradesh to Gurudongmar Lake in Sikkim — CrowdWise India provides real-time crowd-level predictions, hourly forecasts, monthly crowd calendars, and browser push alerts, entirely free of charge and with no user registration required. The platform's prediction engine combines seven weighted signals: time-of-day patterns (22 destination category curves), day-of-week factors, month-level seasonality, Indian public holidays and festivals, Wikipedia page-view trends as tourist-interest proxies, hotel demand patterns, and live weather conditions. The result is a crowd level — Low, Moderate, Heavy, or Overcrowded — updated on every page visit. CrowdWise India launched in 2026 and is built on vanilla JavaScript for maximum portability, deployed globally via Cloudflare Pages for sub-second load times across India. The backend API (Node.js / PostgreSQL on AWS) powers data collection and the gamified feedback loop through which user crowd reports continuously improve prediction accuracy. The platform is free to use at crowdwise.in with no paywall, no advertisements, and no data collection from users.

## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How accurate are the predictions?**
A: Our crowd predictions combine historical patterns, seasonal trends, weather data, and real-time signals to provide 65-75% accuracy. The system improves with user feedback.

**Q: How do you make money if it's free?**
A: Currently, this is a public service project. Future monetization may include premium features for tour operators or partnerships with travel businesses.

**Q: Do I need to download an app?**
A: No! CrowdWise India is a web-based platform. Just visit crowdwise.in from any browser.

**Q: How often is data updated?**
A: Crowd predictions are updated every hour. Weather data refreshes in real-time.

**Q: Can I contribute feedback?**
A: Yes! There's a feedback button on the site. Your input helps improve prediction accuracy.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: The site requires an internet connection to fetch live data, but you can bookmark destinations for quick access.

**Q: What destinations are covered?**
A: We cover 224+ major tourist destinations including:
- UNESCO World Heritage Sites
- Popular temples and religious sites
- Hill stations and beaches
- National parks and wildlife sanctuaries
- Historical monuments
- Popular city attractions
